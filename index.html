Model assumptions and limitations

This application uses a two-dimensional numerical model. It does not include Earth rotation, wind, lift, guidance corrections, terrain, or full six-degree-of-freedom vehicle motion.

Projectile drag should oppose the complete velocity vector. Rocket drag changes with atmospheric density, speed, altitude, and Mach number.

The simulator is intended for education and visualization rather than real-world targeting, navigation, or engineering certification.
